Notes on Order Types:

  • Standard orders submitted through IdealPro are subject to minimum and maximum size restrictions. Customers who wish to submit orders in quantities greater than the standard order maximums can request to gain access to IBs Large-Size Order Facility. In addition to allowing submission of FX orders greater than the stated limits, the Large-Size Order Facility allows customers to RFQ (Request for Quote) for an FX pair specific to the order quantity entered. Please contact Client Services or your IB Sales Representative to see if you qualify for the Large-Size Order Facility.
  • Click here for the forex minimum/maximum order size chart and for special large-size order services.
  • IDEAL FX trades executed after 17:00 ET may be considered the next days trades for statement purposes.
  • IDEAL FX trades in NZD executed after 07:00 NZST/NZDT may be considered the next days trades for statement purposes.
  • Market is inactive from the close of Friday until the opening on Sunday.
  • Quotes are provided by Interbank Dealer(s).
  • In addition to forex cash trading, we also offer CME forex futures trading.
  • Click here for the Forex Holiday Calendar.
  • IDEAL FX includes a suite of FX execution services including IDEAL and IDEALPRO.
