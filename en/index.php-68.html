Getting Started at IBKR

Our award-winning platforms offer everything from powerful trading features for professional traders to streamlined solutions for beginners. See which platform is best for you.

New

IBKR Desktop

Trading Skills: Beginner Intermediate

Our newest client-driven desktop trading platform combines the most popular tools from our flagship Trader Workstation (TWS) with a growing suite of original features suggested by our clients. Trade stocks, options, futures, and more on over 150 markets worldwide from this easy-to-use platform, and continue to enjoy IBKR's great pricing, order execution, research, and market data services.

Download IBKR Desktop Learn About IBKR Desktop

IBKR Trader Workstation (TWS)

Trading Skills: Intermediate Advanced

Our flagship desktop platform designed for seasoned, active traders who trade multiple products and require power and flexibility. Trade stocks, options, futures, currencies, bonds and funds on over 150 markets worldwide from a single unified platform.

Download TWS Learn About TWS

IBKR Mobile

Trading Skills: Intermediate Advanced

For experienced traders who need the power to trade stocks, options, futures, currencies, bonds, and more across more than 150 markets worldwide. No matter where you are, you can access it all with advanced order types and trading tools.

Download IBKR Mobile Learn About IBKR Mobile

IBKR GlobalTrader

Trading Skills: Beginner Intermediate

A streamlined experience to trade stocks, ETFs and options worldwide. Invest with as little as $1 and trade in fractions. Open a simulated trading account in seconds and try IBKR GlobalTrader before opening a live trading account.

Download IBKR Global Trader Learn About IBKR GlobalTrader

IBKR Client Portal
