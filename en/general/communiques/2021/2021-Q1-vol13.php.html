Introducing the No Transaction Fee
Program for Exchange-Traded Funds


Interactive Brokers has launched a "fee waived" no transaction fee (NTF) program1 for exchange-traded funds (ETFs). IBKR will reimburse IBKR Pro clients and eligible non-US clients for commissions paid on eligible ETF shares that are held for at least 30 days.

The first fund families to participate in the program are WisdomTree® Funds, Global X and KraneShares, and we expect to add more funds during the first half of 2021.

WisdomTree Asset Management, Inc. ("WisdomTree"), is an exchange-traded fund ("ETF") sponsor and asset manager headquartered in New York. WisdomTree offers products covering equities, fixed income, currencies, commodities and alternative strategies. For more information, please visit www.wisdomtree.com or follow us on Twitter @WisdomTreeETFs. WisdomTree® is the marketing name for WisdomTree Investments, Inc. and its subsidiaries worldwide.

Global X Management Company LLC is a New York-based provider of exchange-traded funds that facilitates access to investment opportunities across the global markets. The firm currently manages exchange-traded funds that target Commodity Producers, Developed Markets, Emerging Markets, Special Opportunities and Income Producers. For more information, please visit www.globalxetfs.com.

Krane Funds Advisors, LLC is the investment manager for KraneShares ETFs. Our suite of China focused ETFs provide investors with solutions to capture China's importance as an essential element of a well-designed investment portfolio. For more information, please visit www.kraneshares.com.

As a reminder, IBKR Lite clients already pay USD 0 commissions on ETFs.
In addition, please note that IBKR may receive compensation from participating funds.

To browse availability, click the Learn More button or visit the Services > No Transaction Fee ETFs menu from the Interactive Brokers home page.

Interactive Advisors Offers Nearly 80 Portfolios with Low Minimums and Fees

Interactive Advisors is one of the first robo advisors to come to market. The firm, which is a subsidiary of the Interactive Brokers Group, provides US clients with the convenience of online investing with human help, and provides access to a wide range of actively and passively managed portfolios.

Interactive Advisors currently offers clients the ability to select from nearly 80 portfolios, including 18 Socially Responsible Investing (SRI) portfolios. The firm uses a risk questionnaire to match a portfolio's risk with a client's risk profile based on the client's investment objectives, risk tolerance and financial capacity. In addition, clients can use various exclusion groups to avoid companies that they feel do not share their values or add single stock exclusions which automatically apply to all their investments. The firm ensures that detailed business classification and Environmental Social and Governance (ESG) metrics are used in the exclusion group construction process and each exclusion group typically comprises 25 stocks and is reviewed annually.

US-based clients of Interactive Brokers can quickly open an Interactive Advisors advisory account by clicking on the link below or click Learn More to visit the Interactive Advisors website.
