Introducing the Impact Dashboard


In 2018, we introduced Refinitiv Environmental, Social and Corporate Governance (ESG) metrics to provide transparency into the non-financial effects of a company's policy, and recently enhanced our ESG offering to notify you when companies engage in activities counter to your values.

In 2019, Interactive Advisors, which was named "Best for Socially Responsible Investing" in 2020 by Investopedia, launched Socially Responsible Investment Portfolios and now offers 18 ready-made portfolios. Interactive Advisors also lets clients select from exclusion groups to avoid companies that do not align with their values and micro-customize their portfolios with single-stock exclusions.

In 2020, we are introducing the Impact Dashboard to help clients evaluate and invest in companies that align with their values. The Impact Dashboard allows investors to select their personal investment criteria from 13 impact values and principles, including:

  • Clean Air
  • Pure Water
  • Ocean Life
  • Land Health
  • Consumer Safety
  • Ethical Leadership
  • Gender Equality
  • Racial Equality
  • LGBTQ Inclusion
  • Company Transparency
  • Sustainable Product Lifecycle
  • Mindful Business Models
  • Fair Labor & Thriving Communities

Clients can also exclude investments based on 10 categories:

  • Animal Testing
  • Business Ethics Controversies
  • Corporate Political Spending and Lobbying
  • Energy-Intensive
  • Fossil Fuels
  • Greenhouse Emissions
  • Hazardous Waste
  • High Water Usage
  • Tobacco, Alcohol and Gambling
  • Weapons and Gun Manufacturers

Use the Impact Dashboard to evaluate and adjust your portfolio to invest in companies whose responsible world view coincides with your own. Choose the impact goals and principles that are most important to you and then let the Impact Dashboard analyze and grade your portfolio on how your positions align with your principles. The dashboard even shows how each individual investment is helping or hurting your goal of responsible investing. By viewing companies through an "impact lens" of your design, you can clearly see whether their socially responsible goals and principles line up with yours.

The Impact Dashboard is available in Trader Workstation (TWS), IBKR Mobile and Client Portal.

Calibrate Your Portfolio
with Goal Tracker

Interactive Advisors' Goal Tracker projects the hypothetical performance of your portfolio and monitors how likely you are to achieve your goals. You can adjust your inputs – such as monthly contribution amount, goal target date, the cost or outflow associated with the goal – and see how the likelihood of achieving your goals changes.

Why Run Simulations?
The future is unpredictable. Markets are volatile and future portfolio value can vary significantly, which is why it is important to consider several different return paths that your portfolio can take. Some paths will result in performance that exceeds expectations, while other paths may result in performance that disappoints. Goal Tracker uses a commonly used statistical simulation technique called Monte Carlo to simulate a range of paths for portfolio performance, based on long-term return estimates and annual volatility around those estimates.

Goal Tracker is a free tool for Interactive Advisors clients. If you would like to create an Interactive Advisors account to use Goal Tracker or invest in any of the 80+ portfolios it offers, simply log in to Client Portal and access the Interactive Advisors menu item.
